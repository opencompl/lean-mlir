    def Lean.Parser.ppAllowUngrouped :
    Lean.Parser.Parser

    No-op parser combinator that allows the pretty printer to omit the group and indent operation in the enclosing category parser.

    syntax ppAllowUngrouped "by " tacticSeq : term
